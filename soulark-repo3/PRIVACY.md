# SoulArk Desktop Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

SoulArk Desktop ("the App") is a macOS application that allows users to interact with AI agents powered by OpenRouter's API.

## Data We Collect
We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The App runs locally on your device.

## API Keys
Your OpenRouter API key is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to us or any third party other than OpenRouter directly.

## Third-Party Services
The App connects to OpenRouter (openrouter.ai) to process AI requests. Please review OpenRouter's privacy policy at openrouter.ai/privacy.

## Your Data
All conversations and agent files remain on your local machine. We have no access to them.
